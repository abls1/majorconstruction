Major Construction

Services

Major Construction has developed a broad scope of of construction services for both residential and commercial construction projects. Our experience having been involved in many complex projects has allowed the firm to become proficient, detail orientated, and cost effective in all facets of the industry.

To achieve our goal of being a full service contractor we have assembled a team of experienced, trusted, and responsible craftsmen and administrative staff. This has given our firm the ability to take on projects of all scope and size.

General Construction Services

Supply qualified tradesman and labor to perform required construction services

Provide experienced managers for oversight of construction means, methods, sequencing and procedures

Coordinating and supervising work performed by trades for quality, completeness, and conformity with plans and specifications

Coordination with municipal authorities, governmental agencies, and utility companies

Expediting and securing all permits, licenses, inspections, sign-offs, occupancy certificates required by jurisdictional agencies
